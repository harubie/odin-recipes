Spicy Thai Fried Rice

Thai fried rice (Thai: ข้าวผัด, RTGS: khao phat, pronounced [kʰâ(ː)w pʰàt]) is a variety of fried rice typical of central Thai cuisine. In Thai, khao means "rice" and phat means "of or relating to being stir-fried."

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 cup peanut oil for frying
  • 6 large cloves garlic clove, crushed
  • 2 serrano peppers, crushed
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

Directions

Step 1

  1. Gather all ingredien
  2. Whisk together oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar in a small bowl

Step 2

    Heat oil in wok over medium-high heat until oil begins to smoke
